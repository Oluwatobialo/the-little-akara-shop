About LAS

LAS The Little Akara Shop is a small, family-owned business that specializes in making delicious and authentic West African cuisine. Located in the heart of the city, our shop offers a variety of freshly made Akara - a popular West African snack made from black-eyed peas and spices. We take pride in using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients to create our dishes, and our friendly staff are always ready to serve you with a smile. Come visit us today and experience the unique and flavorful taste of The Little Akara Shop!
